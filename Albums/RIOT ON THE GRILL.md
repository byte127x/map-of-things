RIOT ON THE GRILL is [[ELLEGARDEN]]'s 4th studio album. It was released on April 20, 2005 (in Japan) under the GROWING UP label. It reached #3 on the Oricon.
Released by [[Dynamord]] (Japan) and Denka Secca (USA).
